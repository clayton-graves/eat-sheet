So why even diet?

Research shows a multitude of benefits both physical and mental.

Energy you need to keep active throughout the day

Nutrients you need for growth and repair, helping you to stay strong and healthy and help to prevent diet-related illness

Reduce your risk of heart disease by maintaining blood pressure and cholesterol levels

Keeps your teeth and bones strong and can help to slow bone loss (osteoporosis) associated with getting older

Can help you maintain a steady weight

Popular diets.

Ketogenic diet: Less than 50g of carbs per day.

Zone diet: Meals consisting of 40 percent carbs, 30 percent protein and 30 percent fats.

Bulk diet: Daily intake of grams protein equivalent to weight in pounds of person who is dieting.

Paleo diet: Any food that our hunter-gatherer ancestors would have eaten. No dairy, grains, legumes, refined sugars, or processed foods.
